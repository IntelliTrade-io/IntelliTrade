# Third-Party Notices

## Natural Earth

The bundled world basemap used by IntelliConflict is derived from Natural Earth data.
Natural Earth is available at https://www.naturalearthdata.com/ and its datasets are
provided in the public domain.

This notice applies to the bundled world basemap data file in this repository:
`data/world.topo.json`.

The IntelliConflict team transformed and simplified the source geography for local app
bundling and SVG rendering, but the underlying basemap source remains Natural Earth.
